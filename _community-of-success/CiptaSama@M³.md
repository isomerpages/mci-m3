<p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Ciptasama-website-header.jpeg"></div><p>As part of Singapore Together Emerging Stronger Conversations, CiptaSama@M³ provides a platform for the Malay/Muslim community to come together, reflect on what we have learnt from our COVID-19 experience, and chart the way forward as we progress together with the rest of Singapore. This is an important part of our endeavour to build up our Community of Success.</p><p>In January 2021, two Singapore Together Emerging Stronger Conversations x CiptaSama@M³ sessions were held in Malay and English, involving more than 100 Singaporeans.</p><p>On 7 Jan 2021, we discussed the topic of “Seizing Opportunities in the Age of Digital Transformation” with Minister Maliki Osman and Senior Minister of State Zaqy Mohamad.</p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Ciptasama-photo-1.png"></div><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Thank-you-1.jpg"></div><p>On 12 Jan 2021, we discussed the topic of “Strengthening Malay/Muslim Families” with Minister of State Faishal Ibrahim and Parliamentary Secretary Rahayu Mahzam.</p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Ciptasama-photo-2.jpg"></div><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/thank-you-2.jpg"></div><p>Watch the highlights of the sessions <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=78tzB2lFKB8" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>here</u></a>.</p><p>Following these CiptaSama@M³ sessions, we took back the ideas and suggestions, are studying them, and will build a network of community partners who will co-create a better tomorrow for Singapore through the Singapore Together Alliances for Action (AfA).</p><p>Read the report from the ESCs here.</p>
