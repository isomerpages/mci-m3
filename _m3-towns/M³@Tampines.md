<p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/m3-tampines.jpeg"></div><p>M³@Tampines was formed in August 2018 to streamline the efforts of the five Malay Activity Executive Committees (MAEC) in Tampines in meeting the needs of the Malay/Muslim community in Tampines. Together with representatives from MUIS and MENDAKI, M³@Tampines works with the Malay/Muslim professionals to be a one-stop centre for local programmes dedicated in meeting the residents’ ever-changing needs.</p><p>M³@Tampines’ areas of focus include Marriage, Parenthood and Early Childhood. It aims to scale up KelasMateMatika (KMM)@ Tampines West Community Club to benefit more families and young children. M³@Tampines actively works with a network of partners and volunteers to support vulnerable individuals as well as empower and mentor youths.</p><p>Follow M³@Tampines on <a
