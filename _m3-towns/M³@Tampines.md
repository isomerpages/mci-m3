<p>Since its establishment in 2018, M<sup>3</sup>@Tampines has been a cornerstone
of support for the Malay/Muslim community in Tampines, with a particular
focus on residents in the estate's 14 rental blocks.</p>
<p>At the heart of this initiative are its volunteers, the true pillars of
M<sup>3</sup>@Tampines, who are dedicated to helping vulnerable segments
of the community achieve a better future for their families. These committed
individuals conduct house visits to assess needs and connect households
with relevant agencies and partners.</p>
<p>In 2023, M<sup>3</sup>@Tampines launched Project Dian, aiming to support
over 100 Malay-Muslim families in achieving financial stability and educational
goals. The organisation also runs seasonal programmes like Sinar Ramadan
and Kotak Riang, where volunteers distribute meals, groceries, and festive
items to households in need. M<sup>3</sup>@Tampines extends its impact
through educational initiatives such as the bilingual PermataKu reading
programme for pre-schoolers and health-focused activities like the Juanita
exercise programme for women.</p>
<p>Looking ahead, M<sup>3</sup>@Tampines plans to concentrate on Project
Dian and other initiatives that foster resilience, self-reliance, and confidence
within the community, always driven by the tireless efforts of its dedicated
volunteers.</p>
