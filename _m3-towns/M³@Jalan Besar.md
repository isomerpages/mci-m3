<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/PHOTO-2022-09-05-16-11-53.jpg"></div><p>M³@Jalan Besar believes that the community needs to be progressive as they confront the challenges, changes, and diversity in our society. This will ensure a resilient and adaptive attitude towards their goals in life. It is important to inculcate an inclusive and contributive mindset in order to be a significant part of our social ethos and identity in Singapore.</p><p>Launched in February 2021, M³@Jalan Besar aims to build a strong network of partners and volunteers to support vulnerable families, with a strong emphasis on mental health and digital literacy. M³@Jalan Besar was formed to streamline the efforts of the seven Malay Activity Executive Committees (MAEC) and 14 mosques in Jalan Besar GRC in meeting the needs of the Malay/ Muslim community in the GRC.</p><p>Together with representatives from MUIS and MENDAKI, M³@Jalan Besar strives to work with the Malay/Muslim professionals to be a one-stop centre for local customised programmes dedicated in meeting the residents’ ever-changing needs. One of such programmes is the ongoing Project Kukoh which targets to uplift the quality of life of residents in Jalan Kukoh through a collaboration with Masjid Omar Kampong Melaka.
