<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/m3-toa-payoh.jpeg"></div><p>One of the youngest towns at present, M³@Toa Payoh was launched in April 2021 to bring together more professionals, mosque leaders, community leaders and youth to tap on their strengths and expertise to uplift the Malay/Muslim community in Bishan-Toa Payoh GRC &amp; Marymount SMC.</p><p>In particular, this town hopes to enhance residents’ financial literacy and economic resilience as well as to engage vulnerable residents, especially those who reside in rental blocks to understand their needs and aspirations.
