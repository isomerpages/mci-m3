<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/m3-geylang.jpeg"></div><p>Established in January 2021, M³@Geylang Serai prides itself in reigniting the passion and interest in Malay culture and tradition. With the vision set by Mayor Fahmi Aliman, M³@Geylang Serai aims to improve community building through the delivery of relevant programmes under the key pillars of: Creating a Caring Community, Promotion of a Healthy Living mindset and Inculcating the mantra of Lifelong Learning.</p><p>Since its launch, the volunteers in this town have conducted M³ Household Surveys (MHS) to understand the needs and concerns of the Malay Muslim community and to link the families up with the relevant agencies for assistance.</p><p>Follow M³@Geylang Serai on <a href="https://facebook.com/M3atGeylangSerai"
