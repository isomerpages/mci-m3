<p>Since its establishment in January 2021, M³@Geylang Serai prides itself
as a focal point for Malay cultural, educational, and social activities.</p>
<p>M³@Geylang Serai aims to promote both social cohesion and mobility, through
the delivery of relevant programmes and projects, aligned to the 5 Focus
Areas. This is achieved through strategic collaboration with various agencies
and organisations.</p>
<p>We continue to seek like-minded individuals stepping forward and making
a difference.</p>
