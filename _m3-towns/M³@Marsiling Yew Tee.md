<p>Since its inception in July 2019, M<sup>3</sup>@Marsiling-Yew Tee has
been engaging the Malay Muslim community in providing last-mile help to
reach out to families in need and serve the needs and aspirations of the
MM residents in the GRC, with the desired outcomes of strengthening support
for the MM community and to progress together in a meritocracy that is
inclusive and collaborative.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The success of the town’s M<sup>3</sup> programmes lie in the continuous
support of the agencies and networks within the community – school partners,
religious partners, community partners, and informal networks. To achieve
the desired outcomes, M<sup>3</sup>@Marsiling-Yew Tee curate programmes
for the different Focus Areas including Support for Marriage, Parenthood
and Early Childhood (Focus Area 1), Empowerment and Mentoring Youths (Focus
Area 3), and the recently added Focus Area 5 on Health.</p>
