<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/m3-woodlands.jpeg"></div><p>M³@Woodlands was launched by Grassroots Adviser Mr Amrin Amin on 21 July 2019. M³@Woodlands aims to reach out to the Malay Community in Sembawang GRC to build strong family and community, increase health literacy and improve financial wisdom.</p><p>One of its programmes is KelasMateMatika (KMM)@ Admiralty, Sembawang and Woodlands which aims to strengthen the academic readiness of families with pre-school children. Canberra and Gambas will also organise similar KMM@CC programmes to benefit more families and young children.</p><p>M³@Woodlands will continue to work with a network of partners, professionals and volunteers to support the Malay community in Sembawang GRC. M³@Woodlands will also organise programmes for the vulnerable families as well as empower and mentor youths.</p><p>Follow M³@Woodlands on <a href="https://www.facebook.com/M3atWoodlands" rel="noopener
