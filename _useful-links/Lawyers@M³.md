<p>Lawyers@M³ are lawyers, legal professionals, and individuals with legal
backgrounds who grouped together to do what they can for the community
under the auspices of M³. ‍ Together, they hope to improve legal literacy
and awareness in the community and help the underprivileged to better access
legal services. To that end, they conduct talks, pro bono legal clinics,
and sessions where they will share their views and know-how.</p>
<p>Lawyers@M³’s mission is a constant work in progress but one that they
meet with enthusiasm and deep sincerity.</p>
