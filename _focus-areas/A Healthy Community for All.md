<h3><strong>Jaga Kesihatan, Jaga Ummah</strong></h3>
<p>The Jaga Kesihatan, Jaga Ummah (JKJU) <em>(Look after your health, look after your community)</em> network
is made up of representatives from M<sup>3</sup>@Towns, MUIS, MENDAKI,
Malay Activity Executive Committees (MAECs), mosques, Malay/Muslim Organisations,
Muslim Healthcare Professionals Association (Singapore), healthcare sectors,
and other Whole-of-Government and voluntary welfare organisations.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The network offers a wide range of face-to-face and virtual health promoting
programmes to improve the health and well-being of the residents and congregants,
supporting the Malay/Muslim community in leading healthy lifestyles. These
include Community Physical Activity Programmes, healthier cooking demonstrations
and functional screening for seniors as well as talks on smoking cessation
and mental well-being.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The JKJU network empowers its partners to share best practices, address
key health concerns and collaborate to encourage healthy habits within
the Malay/Muslim community.</p>
